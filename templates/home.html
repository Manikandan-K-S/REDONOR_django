About REDONOR

Welcome to REDONOR, the platform dedicated to blood donation. Blood donation is a selfless act that helps save lives. By donating blood, you can make a significant impact on the health and well-being of others in need. REDONOR is a blood donation platform dedicated to connecting blood donors with those in need. Together, we can make a difference in saving lives.

Why Donate Blood?

Save Lives

Donating blood can save lives in emergency situations, surgeries, and treatments for various medical conditions.

Health Benefits

Regular blood donation can help improve cardiovascular health and reduce the risk of certain diseases.

Who Can Donate Blood?

Almost anyone in good health can donate blood. However, there are a few criteria to meet:

  • You must be at least 18 years old.
  • Your weight should be above 50 kg.
  • You should not have any transmittable diseases.

If you meet these requirements, you can become a blood donor and make a difference in someone's life.
